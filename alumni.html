GirlsGoIT Alumni

I am presenting GirlsGoIT alumni initiative at user-centred design workshop at ambassadors leadership meeting in October 2021

Problem Statement

GirlsGoIT is the largest community program in Moldova empowering girls in IT and STEM. It aims at starting an Alumni Association with the goal to advance its programme reach through meaningful, lifetime engagement of its graduates and community members.

Since the programme launched in 2015, the member base keeps growing, with a minimum of 240 girls graduating GirlsGoIT non-formal education yearly. These graduates are currently or about advancing their education in higher institutions and careers in IT.

With the increasing numbers of graduates, the alumni initiative will build an outreach engagement program that connects alumni locally, globally and virtually and targeted programming that are desirable and co-created with and for members of alumni and community of GirlsGoIT but not limited to, ongoing education, professional development, volunteer opportunities.

Design goal:

I was tasked to launch GirlsGoIT alumni initiative, acquire financial resources to pilot activities and project management.

Design steps:

  • Research: Competitive benchmarking to emulate alumni best practices. design thinking workshop organised with GirlsGoIT community members
  • Analysis: Triangulation of competitive benchmarking and user-centred design workshop
  • Design & Fundraising: Concept development, fundraising, task team and implementation
  • Alumni update: Report and state of affairs of product

Role:

Research and development, Project sponsor

Duration:

Sept 2021 - Oct 2022

Research

Competitive benchmarking

To conceptualise GirlsGoIT alumni, I needed information about alumni programs around the world and their conventions and practices. I analysis 3 alumni programs, what they share in common and to be emulated. In the slides below, you will find conventions i benched-marked.

Design thinking workshop

To develop the best alumni experience it is important to put the user into the centre of planning and development. I organised an interactive session with GirlsGoIT members to suggest ideas for the development of alumni initiave based on 3 focus areas and i asked them to write their ideas in sticky paper and post them:

  • Alumni for Alumni
  • Alumni for GirlsGoIT community
  • Alumni for girls/women in Moldova
