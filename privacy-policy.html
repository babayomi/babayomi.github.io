Privacy Policy

Updated: August 26, 2020

GirlsGoIT is a community program empowering girls in IT. Our mission is to build the largest community of girls in IT in Moldova. Read more about Our Mission. GirlsGoIT is founded and implemented by TEKEDU, a not-for-profit organisation registered under the Moldovan law as A.O. Asociatia pentru Dezvoltarea Tehnologiilor Informationale "EDUCAT" (A.O. EDUCAT), with state identification number 1014620000936.

This page informs you of GirlsGoIT and TEKEDU ( “GirlsGoIT”, "TEKEDU", “us”, “we”, or “our”) policies regarding the collection, use and disclosure of "Personal Information" when you use the GirlsGoIT or TEKEDU’s website. We will not use or share your information with anyone except as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information we collect

Personal information

We collect “Personal Information” which is "name and email", which you submit to us through “Sign up for our newsletter and get the latest news”. Your “Personal Information” is uploaded in JungleMail365 and Mailchimp for our email newsletter communications and for the purpose of sending latest news, events and announcement. We do not sell, trade, rent or otherwise share your "Personal Information" with third parties.

Registration form

GirlsGoIT organises education workshops during spring, summer and autumn. During the process of implementing our educational workshops, we announce “Open call for registration and participation” for interested target groups (girls aged between 14 to 20) to apply for the educational workshop. The information we collect are “Full name”, “Age”, “Address”, “Name of high school”, “Location”, "Contact information of Parents or Guardians”, “Knowledge of basic English and IT skills”. This information are collected as part of participants' selection process for acceptance into our spring, summer and autumn educational workshops. Information are collected through Google Forms on TEKEDU’s G-suite account. We do not sell, trade, rent or otherwise share your information provided by "Registration form" with third parties.

Your rights regarding the use of your "Personal information" and "Registration form"

You have the right at any time to prevent us from contacting you through our “Newsletter”. When we send a promotional communication to a user, the user can opt out of further promotional communications by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in each promotional email. Please note that we may also continue to send you administrative emails including, for example, periodic updates to our Privacy Policy.

Links to other websites

We may provide links to or with other websites or applications. However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices employed by those websites or the information or content they contain. This "Privacy Policy" applies solely to information collected by us through our website. Therefore, this Privacy Policy does not apply to your use of a third party website accessed by selecting a link on our website. To the extent that you access or use the website through or on another website, then the privacy policy of that other website will apply to your access or use of that website. We encourage our users to read the privacy statements of other websites before proceeding to use them.

Changes to our "Privacy Policy"

TEKEDU reserves the right to change this “Privacy Policy” at any time. We will notify you of significant changes to our “Privacy Policy” by sending a notice to the email address registered through “Personal Information” or “Registration form”.

Contact us

If you have any questions regarding this "Privacy Policy" or the practices of GirlsGoIT, please contact us by sending an email to girlsgoit@tekedu.org.
