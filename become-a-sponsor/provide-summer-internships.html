Introduction to GirlsGoIT summer camp and internship

About GirlsGoIT

Founded in 2015, we are a community programme with a network of local clubs present in 17 regions in Moldova. We are empowering girls especially in rural communities to improve their soft skills, digital, tech and engineering skills. Our priorities are providing access to education where there are no opportunities, mentoring and advocating for girls to pursue career and education in tech. Learn more about us.

About the summer camp

GirlsGoIT is a 10-days summer camp programme organised for girls to improve their soft skills, learn how to build static and dynamic website, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, 3D Printing and Electronics. 60 girls between the ages of 14 to 20 are selected from around 20 regions in Moldova to participate in the camp program. GirlsGoIT camp started in summer 2015 and have trained more than 294 during the summer camp workshop activity. Watch the playlist of our summer camp videos.

Our courses

Since 2015, we built and have been updating GirlsGoIT curriculum which has training lessons developed on: Front-end Static web development, Front-end Dynamic web development, Back-end web development, 3D printing and Robotics.

About summer internship

During the summer camp, visits to tech companies and internships are organised in partnership with companies to provide best experience possible so they can become more confident in themselves, acquire digital skills and pursue career opportunities in digital jobs.

GirlsGoIT facilitated summer internship experiences of 25 young women in partnership with Ellation, MoldData, Atomate, Amigo, FFW Agency, DAS Solutions and World Bank in Washington DC. Watch our internship videos

About the internship process

The process has 4 components: preparation for internship, selection of interns, on-boarding interns and internship experience and evaluation. At this stage, we will start with preparing your company to provide internships.

Structure of internship

You can choose to provide in-office or remote internships. However works for your company and most convenient for your business operations and employees.

Internship duration and timeline

The standard in-office duration of internship is 1 month, and we recommend it start immediately after summer camp ends because participants must return to their schools. For remote internship, it is possible for more than 1-month duration (max 2 months), only if both parties (participants + your company) reach a common timeline agreement. We will also keep the parents of the participants aware. TEKEDU (implementing GirlsGoIT) will make sure of this.

Preparation for internship

    1. Fill in the summer internship form
    2. A member of TEKEDU team will contact you as soon as we receive your company's internship forms
    3. Sign an internship cooperation agreement with TEKEDU and assign an internship account manager
    4. Draft a press release about summer internship cooperation. Press release will be coordinated with your company
