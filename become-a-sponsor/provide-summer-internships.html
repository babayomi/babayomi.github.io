Introduction to GGIT camp and internship

GGIT is a 10-days summer camp programme organised for young women to improve their soft skills, learn how to build static and dynamic website, using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, 3D Printing and Electronics. 60 girls between the ages of 14 to 20 are selected from around 20 regions in Moldova to participate in the camp program. GGIT camp started in summer 2015 and have trained more than 294 during the summer camp workshop activity. Watch the playlist of our summer camp videos.

About GGIT summer internship

During the summer camp, visits to tech companies and internships are organised in partnership with companies to provide best experience possible so they can become more confident in themselves, acquire digital skills and pursue career opportunities in digital jobs.

GGIT facilitated summer internship experiences of 25 young women in partnership with Ellation, MoldData, Atomate, Amigo, FFW Agency, DAS Solutions and World Bank in Washington DC. Watch our internship videos!

About the internship process

We have built a smarter way to get your company on board for the summer internship programme

We have made it internship can be or It is flexible that the internship your company is providing can either be in-office or remote internship. However works for your company and most importantly convenient for your business operations and employees.

Internship duration and timeline

The standard in-office duration of internship is 1 month, and we recommend it start immediately after summer camp ends (end date camp), since participants must return to their schools. For remote internship, it is possible for more than 1-month duration (max 2 months), only if both parties (participants + your company) reach a common understanding. We will also keep the parents of the participants aware. TEKEDU (implementing ggit) will make sure of this.

Preparation for internship

It starts with expectations drawn to identify standards and skills required by your company in other to provide an internship. These expectations are important and will be added to the learning programme of the 10 days programme.

    1. Fill in the summer internship form
    2. A member of TEKEDU team will contact you as soon as we receive your organisation internship forms
    3. Sign an internship cooperation agreement with TEKEDU and assign an internship account manager
    4. Draft a press release about summer internship cooperation. Press release will be coordinated with your company
