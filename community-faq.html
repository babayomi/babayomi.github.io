What is GirlsGoIT

  • GirlsGoIT is also known as "GGIT" in abbreviation so whenever you see it in contents we publish just know that it is GirlsGoIT. You can also use "GGIT" as #Hashtag for example; #ggit or #girlsgoit

    GGIT was launched on the 27 of March 2015 in the village of Minjir. Mingir is a commune in Hîncești District, Moldova

  • GGIT is a programme empowering girls in IT. It can also be called a community programme because of our activities with girls from rural areas in the region. Currently we operate in 17 regions in Moldova.

    GGIT is an award-winning program. In 2017, we received WITH and FOR girls awards for Europe and Central Asia by the collective of global partners
